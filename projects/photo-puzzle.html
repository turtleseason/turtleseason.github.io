Photo Puzzle

C# Unity Engine Visual Studio

A 2D game based on logic puzzles, with some side-scrolling stages. Play as a photographer seeking out little-known landmarks in a fictional world - solve puzzles to find where the landmarks are located, then go out and visit them.

Made with Unity. Currently the game is at a rough "demo" stage - the art and UI assets are preliminary, but the grid puzzle and one short side-scrolling level are implemented and playable.
